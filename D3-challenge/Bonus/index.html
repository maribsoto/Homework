American's Healthcare Availability & Income

Healthcare vs Income

This chart shows that Healthcare availability and Poverty considerably vary among States for the American population.

Massachusetts has the lowest Lack of Healthcare in US, which is 4.6%. Even though 11.6% of the Population is in Poverty. New Hampshire is the State with the lowest Poverty of 9.2%. However, 11.4 % of Americans Lack Healthcare.

While Texas has Poverty of 17.2%. This is the State with the highest Lack of Healthcare ~25%. Mississippi and New Mexico have the highest Poverty in US, where 18.8% and 15.4% of population lack of Healthcare, respectively.
